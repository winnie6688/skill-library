## Cover generation purpose

Do not copy an existing cover. Generate a new cover concept and image prompt that references the established style.

## Style rules

- Minimal and restrained.
- Spacious with clear hierarchy.
- Low-saturation visual system.
- Technology feeling but not cold.
- Product implementation and real work scene feeling.
- Avoid exaggerated human expressions.
- Avoid poster-like big-character shouting.
- Avoid high-saturation blue-purple, neon colors, and excessive gradients.

## Preferred layout

- Left: strong title.
- Near title: small column label.
- Under title: one-line subtitle.
- Right: product UI, computer, workflow diagram, work scene, or abstract AI/system element.
- Background: ivory beige, sage green, soft moss, or other low-saturation brand colors.
